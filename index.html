ESP32 Zephyr

Build

$ source  [PATH_TO_ZEPHYRPORJECT]/zephyrproject/zephyr/zephyr-env.sh
$ west build -b esp32_devkitc/esp32/procpu -- -DSSID="WIFI_SSID" -DPSK="WIFI_PASS"

where WIFI_SSID is the SSID of the Wifi network that you wish to connect to and WIFI_PASS the authentication for it.

Flash

$ west flash

Monitor

$ west espressif monitor
